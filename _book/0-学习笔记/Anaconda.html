Anaconda

Mac 打开 Jupyter

terminal: python3 -m IPython notebook

配置别名，快捷打开：

echo 'alias notebook="cd && python3 -m IPython notebook"'>> ~/.bash_profile

mac下更改Jupyter notebook的默认工作目录： https://blog.csdn.net/weixin_43289607/article/details/82882581
